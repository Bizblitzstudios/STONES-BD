Our high-quality raw materials form the essential foundation for efficient and high-performing industrial operations.

Our Raw Material Offerings

We provide a diverse selection of industrial-grade raw materials to meet various application requirements:

  • Slag - A byproduct of metal smelting with applications in construction and cement production
  • Iron Oxide - Used in pigments, coatings, and as a raw material in steel production
  • Gypsum - Essential for construction materials like drywall and cement
  • Basalt - A volcanic rock used in construction aggregates and as a raw material for fiber production
  • Quartz - Used in glass manufacturing, electronics, and construction
  • Calcium Carbonate - A versatile mineral with applications in paper, plastics, paints, and construction
  • Limestone - A fundamental material for cement production and construction aggregates
  • Clay - Used in ceramics, construction materials, and various industrial applications
